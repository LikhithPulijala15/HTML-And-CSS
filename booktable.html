Sphagetti Receipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Boil water to boil.
  2. Add spaghetti to the boiling water.
    1. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally
    2. Add marinara sauce to a pan, bringing to a simmer
  3. Add salt to taste
  4. Mix cooked spaghetti with marinara sauce
  5. Enjoy!
